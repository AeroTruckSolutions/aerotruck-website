About AeroTruck Solutions

Built by operators and accountants who know trucking. We deliver operational reliability, regulatory compliance, and financial clarity — so carriers focus on moving freight.

Our story

AeroTruck Solutions started as a small group of fleet managers and CPAs who saw the same problems repeating across carriers: fragmented operations, late paperwork, and wasted driver hours. We built a process-driven service combining modern telematics, disciplined accounting, and human-centered dispatch. The result: fewer disruptions, predictable margins, and drivers who want to stay.

Our approach

We emphasize proactive maintenance planning, detailed route analytics, and continuous regulatory checks. Our technology stack integrates with common ELD providers and telematics vendors while our teams handle the human tasks — driver coaching, payroll, and carrier compliance.
