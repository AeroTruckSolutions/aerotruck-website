About Aerotruck Solutions

We exist to simplify fleet operations for carriers who expect reliability, precision and growth. Our combined operations and finance teams deliver consistent results across large and mid-size fleets.

Our story

Founded by industry veterans, Aerotruck Solutions was built from the road up. Our leaders have decades of experience running operations, managing compliance programs, and building payroll processes for carriers. We saw the same problems repeatedly — fractured data, slow payroll cycles, and reactive compliance. So we built a different model: unified operations + finance, backed by modern automation and proactive account management.

What sets us apart

  • Integrated operations: We connect dispatch, telematics, and payroll into a single workflow.
  • Full accountability: Dedicated account teams and SLA-driven support.
  • Scalable delivery: From single-fleet pilots to multi-regional operations.

Leadership & values

We prioritize safety, compliance, transparency and continuous improvement. Our leadership combines operational know-how with financial discipline to protect margins and keep drivers paid on time.
