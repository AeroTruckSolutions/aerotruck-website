About Aerotruck Solutions

Redefining logistics through innovation, precision, and trust.

Our Mission

At Aerotruck Solutions, we aim to provide world-class operational, dispatch, and administrative support for carriers and drivers across North America. Our team of logistics professionals works tirelessly to ensure every mile counts — optimizing routes, reducing costs, and empowering fleets with real-time insights.

Founded by transportation industry experts, Aerotruck Solutions was built to modernize fleet management. We understand the everyday challenges drivers and owners face, from compliance and accounting to dispatch and communication — and we built our company to solve them all under one roof.

Our Vision

We believe in a connected future where every truck, every driver, and every company is seamlessly integrated into a digital ecosystem that keeps the wheels of logistics running smoother than ever before.
