Services

Full-service fleet management, accounting, payroll, recruiting, and 24/7 operations support tailored to trucking.

Accounting & Payroll

We provide complete bookkeeping, tax reporting (including IFTA), driver payroll, and month-end reconciliation. Our processes are built for the nuances of trucking: variable miles, fuel tax allocation, detention and layover pay, and driver settlements. Detailed reports and dashboards help you keep cashflow predictable and compliant.

Fleet Management

From telematics and preventive maintenance scheduling to routing optimization and fuel program management — we manage the operational picture so your drivers stay on schedule. Real-time exception alerts combined with monthly performance reviews help reduce downtime and lower operating costs.

Recruiting & Driver Services

Our recruiting includes sourcing, CDL screening, background checks, drug testing coordination, and onboarding. We also provide retention programs, training coordination, and driver scorecards to maintain a reliable workforce.

24/7 Dispatch & Roadside Assistance

Round-the-clock dispatch and roadside coordination with trusted vendors — because incidents happen after hours. We manage reroutes, emergency repairs, and customer communication so operations resume with minimal delay.
